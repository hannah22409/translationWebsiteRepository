Process

Quick and Easy


1. Quote

You complete a short questionnaire and submit documents by email. I send you a quote and timeline within 2 business days. Fees start at $50 USD.

2. Payment

You confirm the translation by proceeding with payment. I confirm the delivery date (usually within 3-5 business days).

3. Translation

I translate your document and a second translator reviews it. I prepare a translation package that includes the translation, and a copy of the original.

4. Delivery

I email you a PDF of the translation package. If requested, I ship a hard copy via registered mail or courier.
